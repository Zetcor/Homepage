My Dream Places

Niagara Falls

Why It’s Special to Me

One of the most famous waterfalls in the world. The sheer size and power of the falls make it a must-see, whether from above, from a boat, or even from behind. It’s also one of those places that looks incredible in any season.

How to Get There

  1. From a nearby city: Take a bus or taxi (about 1.5 hours). VIA Rail also has train options.
  2. From a closer town: A bus or taxi ride takes around 30–40 minutes.
  3. By Tour Bus: Many companies offer direct trips from different locations.

Other Places To Visit

Maid of the Mist

A thrilling boat tour that takes you up close to Niagara Falls, where you can feel the mist and hear the roar of the water. Ponchos are a must!

Journey Behind the Falls

Explore tunnels leading to a viewing deck behind Niagara Falls, where you can experience the powerful rush of water up close.

Clifton Hill

A lively entertainment district packed with attractions, including arcades, and the Niagara SkyWheel, offering great views of the falls.
