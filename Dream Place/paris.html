My Dream Places

The Eiffel Tower

Why It’s Special to Me

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most recognizable structures in the world. When it was first built in 1889, many people actually disliked it, but now it’s a symbol of France and romance. Thousands of people visit every day just to see it in person. Some say it looks best at night when it sparkles with lights every hour. I think seeing it in real life would be completely different from just looking at pictures. Many people describe standing underneath it as a surreal experience. Climbing to the top would also be an amazing way to see Paris from above. It’s one of those places that feels like a must-visit, just because of how iconic it has become.

How to Get There

  1. Metro: Take Line 6 to Bir-Hakeim or Line 9 to Trocadéro for a great view.
  2. Bus: Several routes stop nearby.
  3. Boat: A Seine River cruise offers a great perspective.

Other Places To Visit

Louvre Museum

One of the world’s most famous museums, home to thousands of artworks, including Leonardo da Vinci’s Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo. Its iconic glass pyramid entrance is a symbol of art and history.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

A masterpiece of Gothic architecture, known for its stunning stained glass windows, towering spires, and legendary gargoyles. Despite the fire in 2019, it remains a symbol of France’s cultural heritage.

Champs-Élysées & Arc de Triomphe

A grand avenue lined with shops, cafés, and theaters, leading to the Arc de Triomphe, a monument honoring those who fought for France. The top offers a breathtaking panoramic view of the city.
