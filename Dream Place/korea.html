My Dream Places

Namsan Tower

Why It’s Special to Me

This is one of the best spots to get a full view of Seoul. It’s a mix of modern cityscapes and a touch of tradition with Namsan Park surrounding it. The tower itself is a major landmark, and the night view from here is especially impressive.

How to Get There

  1. Cable Car: Take a short walk from Myeongdong Station to the cable car station.
  2. Shuttle Bus: Buses 02, 03, or 05 stop near the tower.

Other Places

Gyeongbokgung Palace

The largest and most iconic royal palace in Korea, built during the Joseon Dynasty. Known for its grand architecture, scenic gardens, and the changing of the guard ceremony.

Bukchon Hanok Village

A charming neighborhood filled with traditional Korean houses (hanok), offering a glimpse into the past. It’s a great place to walk around, take photos, and experience Korea’s cultural heritage.

Myeongdong

A bustling shopping district famous for its trendy fashion, beauty stores, and delicious street food. It’s the go-to spot for skincare lovers and foodies exploring Seoul.
