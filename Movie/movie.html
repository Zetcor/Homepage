Ben 10

Ben 10 (Classic, 2005-2008)

Creators: Man of Action (Duncan Rouleau, Joe Casey, Joe Kelly, Steven T. Seagle)
Stars (Voice Cast):
  1. Tara Strong as Ben Tennyson
  2. Meagan Smith as Gwen Tennyson
  3. Paul Eiding as Grandpa Max
  4. Steve Blum as Vilgax

Synopsis:

Ben Tennyson, a 10-year-old boy, discovers a mysterious alien device called the Omnitrix, which allows him to transform into different alien beings. With the help of his cousin Gwen and his grandfather Max, Ben embarks on adventures to fight villains, extraterrestrial threats, and dangerous criminals. As he learns to master the Omnitrix, he faces powerful foes like Vilgax while also growing as a hero. With his unique abilities and courage, Ben protects the world from evil and saves the day as the superhero Ben 10.

Transformers

Transformers (2007)

Director: Michael Bay
Stars:
  1. Shia LaBeouf as Sam Witwicky
  2. Megan Fox as Mikaela Banes
  3. Josh Duhamel as Captain Lennox
  4. Tyrese Gibson as Sergeant Epps
  5. Peter Cullen (Voice of Optimus Prime)
  6. Hugo Weaving (Voice of Megatron)

Synopsis:

When teenager Sam Witwicky unknowingly buys a car that is actually the Autobot Bumblebee, he becomes entangled in an ancient war between two factions of alien robots: the heroic Autobots, led by Optimus Prime, and the evil Decepticons, led by Megatron. As the battle unfolds on Earth, Sam and his ally Mikaela Banes must help the Autobots recover the powerful AllSpark, a mysterious artifact that grants life to machines. With the fate of humanity at stake, the Autobots and the U.S. military unite in an explosive battle against the Decepticons, leading to a high-stakes showdown that determines the survival of Earth.
