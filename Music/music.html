You'll Be Safe Here

"You'll Be Safe Here" - Rico Blanco

Release Year: 2006
Album: You'll Be Safe Here (EP)
Song Info:
  • A song about protection, comfort, and reassurance, "You'll Be Safe Here" became one of the most iconic OPM songs. It was originally released by Rivermaya and later re-recorded as a solo project by Rico Blanco. The track gained international recognition when it was chosen as the theme song for MTV Asia's "Rouge," proving its universal appeal. The emotional depth in the lyrics and its sweeping instrumental arrangement make it a song that listeners turn to for solace.
Artist Info:
  • Rico Blanco is a renowned Filipino singer-songwriter, musician, and producer. As the former frontman of Rivermaya, he was responsible for some of the band's biggest hits before embarking on a successful solo career. Known for his versatility in music, he has explored various genres from rock to electronic pop. His other famous songs include "Antukin," "Liwanag sa Dilim," and "Yugto."
